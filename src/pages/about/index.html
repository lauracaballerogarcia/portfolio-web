Hello, I'm Laura, a digital Product Designer currently based in Asturias, Spain.

With a background in graphic design, I specialize in digital products and web experiences. My work sits at the intersection of technology, behaviour and impact, from user research and UX strategy to crafting scalable design systems that bring consistency across products and platforms.

I'm well aware that the best solutions often lie beyond my own perspective. That's why I'm a team player (quite literally). I enjoy working with others to challenge ideas, test hypotheses, and build better products together.

Outside of work, I'm curious about the relationship between people and the spaces they inhabit — both physical and digital.

I spend time hiking, reading, and thinking about how design shapes everyday life in ways we rarely notice.
